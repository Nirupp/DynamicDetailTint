Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking. Still have questions? Call or text us.

Most vehicles take 1.5–2 hours for a full tint. Teslas and vehicles with complex rear glass can take 2–3 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book. Plan to leave the vehicle with us for the day.

Appointments are strongly recommended — we're often booked out several days. You can walk in for a quick quote or to ask questions, but we can't always fit same-day work without a reservation. Book online or call (410) 674-2430.

Maryland law requires front side windows to allow at least 35% of light in (VLT). Rear side windows and the rear window can be any darkness on non-sedans. We know Maryland tint laws and will keep your vehicle street-legal. We'll discuss your options when you arrive.

Don't roll your windows down for 3–5 days after installation to let the film cure properly. Avoid cleaning the inside of the windows for 2 weeks. After that, use a soft cloth with ammonia-free cleaner — never abrasive products. Properly maintained tint lasts 10+ years.

System X Sport lasts 1–2 years. System X Diamond lasts 5+ years with proper maintenance. Both come with a decontamination wash and clay bar prep included. We offer maintenance packages to keep your coating performing at its best year after year.

Yes — we offer full and partial vinyl wraps in a wide range of colors and finishes including matte, gloss, satin, chrome, and color-shift. Pricing is custom-quoted based on your vehicle and coverage area. Call or fill out the contact form for a vinyl wrap estimate.

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and Zelle. For larger jobs (ceramic coatings, vinyl wraps, full details), we may require a deposit to hold your appointment. Payment is due upon completion of service.

Absolutely. We offer fleet pricing for businesses with multiple vehicles and commercial window tinting for buildings, storefronts, and offices. Contact us for a custom fleet or commercial quote — we'll come to you for larger projects.
